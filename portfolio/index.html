About

fredrik johansson

I’m a UX/UI Designer based in Gothenburg, with a background that spans both the service industry and the film world. Over the years, I’ve worked across a wide range of roles in film production — from creative tasks to highly structured coordination — which has given me a strong understanding of both storytelling and execution under pressure.

I’m especially interested in how structure and creativity meet in digital products, and I enjoy shaping experiences that feel clear, human, and intuitive. My foundation in UX design helps me translate complex needs into simple, usable solutions.

Outside of work, I like spending time with friends and staying open to whatever the day brings. I’m naturally curious and always looking to experience new things, learn something new, and grow from it. I’m motivated by collaboration and the energy that comes from working with others to solve problems together. I’m also used to fast-paced environments and stay calm and focused even when things get stressful.

Soft skills:

  • Collaboration & teamwork
  • Strong systems thinking (seeing the bigger picture and “ripples”)
  • Stress resilience
  • Curiosity and continuous learning
  • Clear communication
